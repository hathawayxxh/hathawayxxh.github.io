[Curriculum Vitae]


I am currently a Postdoc Fellow in AIM Group, City University of Hong Kong (CityU). I obtained the Ph.D. degree in the Department of Electrical Engineering, CityU in 2022, supervised by Prof. Yixuan Yuan. Prior to that, I received the Master degree in MOE-Microsoft Key Laboratory of Multimedia Computing and Communication, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) in 2019, supervised by Prof. Weiping Li and Prof. Zhibo Chen, and the B.S. degree in Information Engineering, Xi'an Jiaotong University (XJTU) in 2016, with admission to Automation Elite Class of XJTU and CASIA.

My research interests include Medical Image Analysis, Neural Network Acceleration and Machine Learning.
