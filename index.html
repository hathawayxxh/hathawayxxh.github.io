I am currently a Postdoc Fellow in the Department of Radiation Oncology of Stanford University, advised by Prof. Lei Xing. Before joining Stanford, I worked as a Postdoc Fellow in the Department of Electrical Engineering of City University of Hong Kong, advised by Prof. Yixuan Yuan. I obtained the Ph.D. degree in the Department of Electronic Engineering, The Chinese University of Hong Kong in 2021, supervised by Prof. Max Q.-H. Meng and Prof. Hongsheng Li. Prior to that, I received the B.S. degree in Biomedical Engineering, Shandong University in 2017.

My research interests include Deep Learning, Medical Image Analysis, and Omics Analysis.

I am looking for remote collaborators, please drop me an email if you are interested.
